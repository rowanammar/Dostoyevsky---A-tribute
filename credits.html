Tribute to Fyodor Dostoevsky

About the Project

This website is a tribute to Fyodor Dostoevsky, one of the greatest literary minds in history. As a passionate admirer of his works, I have created this platform to celebrate his characters and the profound themes he explored in his novels.

Creator's Note

Hello! I'm Rowan! a Computer Science student with a deep love for literature and a growing interest in web development. This project merges my admiration for Dostoevsky with my enthusiasm for creating digital experiences. I hope this tribute site serves as a resource and inspiration for fellow fans and newcomers alike.
